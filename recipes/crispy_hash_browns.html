Crispy Hash Browns

Photo from an Allrecipes Member of their completed crispy hash browns!

This simple recipe is perfect for those who want to create a delicous breakfast in just 15 minutes. The fan-favorite breakfast addition, potatoes, makes for a simple way to add some flair to your mornings. In this recipe, we will clearly go over all of the steps to ensure that your breakfast is perfect. Please ensure that you read ahead and account that you have all of the ingredients available.

Let's get started!

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium russet potatoes, shredded
  • 1/2 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup oil for frying, or as needed
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Rinse shredded potatoes until water is clear, then drain and squeeze dry.
  3. Place shreds in a bowl and mix in the onion, flour, and egg until evenly distributed.
  4. Heat about 1/4 inch of oil in a large heavy skillet over medium-heat. When oil is sizzling hot, place potatoes into the pan in a 1/2 inch thick layer. Cover the whole bottom of the pan, or make separate piles like pancakes
  5. Cook until nicely browned on the bottom, then flip over and brown on the other side. It should take at least 5 minutes per side. If you are cooking them in one big piece, it can be cut into quarters for easier flipping.
  6. Remove from pan, and drain on paper towels. Season with salt and pepper and serve immediately.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
